Our History

TechCon was founded in 2015 as a small gathering of local developers and innovators in East Africa. What started as a one-day meetup with 120 attendees has grown into one of the continent's leading technology conferences. Over the past nine years, we've hosted more than 15,000 participants from 40+ countries, featuring cutting-edge talks on AI, blockchain, sustainable tech, and digital inclusion.

From humble beginnings in a university auditorium to a multi-day event at premier venues, TechCon continues to evolve while staying true to its mission of making advanced technology accessible and relevant to African challenges and opportunities.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and real-world impact. We believe that innovation should serve communities, solve local problems, and create inclusive economic opportunities.

Our core principles include:

  • Accessibility – making advanced tech knowledge available to underrepresented groups
  • Relevance – focusing on technologies that address African priorities
  • Collaboration – fostering connections between students, startups, enterprises, and researchers
  • Sustainability – promoting ethical and environmentally conscious innovation
